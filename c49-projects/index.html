ABOUT

WhiteHat Jr is a global ed-tech company, connecting students to teachers, worldwide. This is a Live online 1:1 platform that connects 5-12-year-old kids with early childhood Coding and Math experts. Our curriculum is created by IIT/IIM Alumni. Our Mission - AI will touch 800 Million Jobs in 2020. The world will split into creators and consumers. WhiteHat Jr empowers a whole generation to become creators of technology. Products - Free Coding Trial Class: WhiteHat Jr teaches kids the fundamentals of coding - logic, structure, and algorithmic thinking to help kids generate creative outcomes, e.g. animations and apps. Free Math Trial Class: Create With Math aims to make your children Math Confident, by teaching mathematical concepts in the context of everyday scenarios, and leveraging these concepts to create solutions to real world problems.
